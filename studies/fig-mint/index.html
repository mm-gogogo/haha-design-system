Section II — How a Quiet Machine Keeps You Three Habits, One Warm Glow
No. 01

Morning Pages

Wake the screen and it greets you with a blank green field — no notifications, no red badges. Type until the noise settles. Fernroot saves each line to the Mint Disk and never asks you to perform.

By the third paragraph the day usually finds its own quiet order.

No. 02

Tidy Drawers

Loose thoughts go into labelled drawers — Errands, Ideas, Someday, Let Go. The machine sorts gently in the background, the way a librarian re-shelves at closing time, returning what you need before you ask.

Nothing is urgent here. That is the entire point.

No. 03

The Garden View

Every entry grows a small phosphor sprig. Over weeks the screen fills with a modest green garden — proof, in pixels, that you kept showing up. No streaks to break, no guilt when a day is missed.

A record of attention, not a scoreboard.
