Lantern Pier Services Agreement

This sample contract concerns a fictional harbor services pilot between Lantern Pier Works and Sable Harbor Analytics. The review focuses on payment triggers, data access, and termination notices.

4.2 Payment Trigger Fees become payable only after acceptance of the transfer log and written confirmation from both project leads. The clause does not define acceptance evidence for late delivery disputes.
7.1 Confidential Records Operational reports, route models, and identity logs remain confidential for the review period. Disclosure to outside reviewers requires prior written approval.
12.3 Termination Notice Either party may end the pilot with thirty days of notice after a documented service failure and a cure period.
