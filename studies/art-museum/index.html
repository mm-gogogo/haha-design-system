Selected holdings / 馆藏选件

A quiet field of recent entries

Eight imagined works are registered here as studies in proportion, restraint, and the museum grammar of caption, frame, and pause.

SA.26.014

Quiet Square with Ochre Margin

Elian Vora, 2018

Mineral pigment on linen

A centered field study with one warm registration mark.

SA.26.027

Study for a Low Window

Maelle Kint, 2022

Gouache and graphite on paper

The lower band holds the room in a single breath.

SA.26.041

Blue Field, Small Interval

Sorin Hale, 2016

Casein and powdered stone

A blue plane divided by one spare vertical pause.

SA.26.063

The Unlit Corridor

Nira Tesh, 2020

Ink wash on prepared cotton

Perspective is narrowed until the dark becomes architectural.

SA.26.088

Folded Garden in Slate

Oren Vale, 2014

Tempera on rag board

A garden reduced to folded planes and one dense shade.

SA.26.096

A Stone Remembers Water

Celine Arno, 2023

Pigment, lime, and sealed paper

The circular center is held just above a low tide mark.

SA.26.112

Margin for a Sleeping Form

Lio Carven, 2019

Dry pigment and wax on panel

A softened shape rests against a measured red edge.

SA.26.137

Paper Gate at Dusk

Yara Mott, 2017

Layered paper and casein

Two hinged panels make a doorway without a room.
