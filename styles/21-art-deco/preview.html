The Ornament System

Geometry, gilded — never gaudy.

Every flourish earns its place: the sunburst over the stage, the chevron along the cornice, the fine double-gold rule beneath each heading. We borrow the 1920s vocabulary and speak it with restraint — symmetry first, gold sparingly.
